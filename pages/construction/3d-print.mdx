# Construction: 3D Printing

> 3D printing or additive manufacturing is the construction of a three-dimensional object from a CAD model or a digital 3D model.
>
> [Fused deposition modeling (FDM)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fused_filament_fabrication), which uses a continuous filament of a [thermoplastic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thermoplastic) material, is the most common 3D printing process.
