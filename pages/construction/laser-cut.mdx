# Construction: Laser Cutting

- ["Laser cutting" on Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laser_cutting)
- ["An Ultimate Guide on How to Use a Laser Cutter" on WikiHow](https://www.wikihow.com/Use-a-Laser-Cutter)

Laser cutters use a laser to cut or etch 2D materials like plywood or sheet metal.

For everyday people, a laser cutter may be found at your nearby [hackerspace](https://hackerspaces.org/) or [Fablab](https://www.fablabs.io/).
