# Construction: T-Slot Aluminium Extrusion

- ["T-slot structural framing" on Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T-slot_structural_framing)

T-Slot aluminium extrusion is a modular building system based on a t-slot profile of extruded aluminium.

Popularized amongst industry by [80/20](https://8020.net/), was then popularized amongst makers by [OpenBuilds](https://openbuilds.com/).

Comes in a variety of profiles: 2020, 2040, 2060, 2080, 4040, etc. Where 2020 means a 20mm x 20mm profile.
