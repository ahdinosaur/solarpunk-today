# Energy: Batteries

- ["Electric battery" on Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_battery)
- ["Batteries" on Appropedia](https://www.appropedia.org/Batteries)

Batteries store energy generated, for later use.

This can be especially important in a [solar electrical system](./solar), as loads often need energy when the sun is not out, and some days have little sun, and some loads need more power than the panels put out.
